Software Development Life-Cycle (SDLC)

Definition:
    Is a cost & time efficient process that development teams use to develope 
    software.

Dev Ops engineers

Definition:
    Is an IT generalist who should have a wide ranging knowledge of both development and operations

Role in the SDLC
    As for DevOps engineers roles in the SDLC. They consult with all development and operation teams
    to introduce methodologies processes, tools to help align tasks & expectations within the
    scope of the project.
