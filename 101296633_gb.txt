# George Brown College Institute

Vision Statement: Achieving community and economic development through education for the sustained growth of individuals and communities. Delivering on 
the promise of engagement, opportunities, solutions, and strategic models that inform system change. Together, we strengthen individuals and communities through education.

Mission Statement: Leading large scale, collaborative initiatives with multiple partners to provide pathways to post-secondary 
futures. Our College, community, and industry partners, work with us to implement innovative projects.

Location: The College has three campuses located in downtown Toronto

    Casa Loma Campus: 160 Kendal Ave Toronto ON M5R 1M3
    St. James Campus: 200 King Street East Toronto ON M5A 3W8
    Waterfront Campus: 3 Lower Jarvis Street Toronto ON M5E 3Y5
